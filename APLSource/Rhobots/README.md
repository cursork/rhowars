# Built-in Rhobots

These are meant to be demonstrative only, _not_ good.

## Camper

Just stops and keeps firing in a direction.

## Coward

Tries to hide in corners.

## Kamikaze

Once it sees a target, just goes towards them whilst firing.

## RandomWalker

Walks randomly, but will lock on targets it sees.

## Spinner

Tries to get to the centre; constantly spins the turret and fires.
